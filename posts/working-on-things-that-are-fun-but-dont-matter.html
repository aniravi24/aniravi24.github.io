Working On Things That Are Fun, But Don’t Matter

As Ethan Naluz (opens in a new tab) and I keep working on new projects, something we’ve found up until now is that we’ve never worked on something that we deeply cared about. We first worked on a robocall-blocking app called Pepper (opens in a new tab), and then we worked on a product called FoodFeed (opens in a new tab), which was social media for foodies. We also worked on a few other things. While we learned a ton from building and built new skills each time, what we found was that as much as we could code all day, we didn’t feel a deep passion for anything we built. There was no founder-problem fit. We decided to go back to the drawing board and find something that mattered to us.
